Cosmology Calculator

Key quantities
Distances and times

A cosmology quantity calculator for high-redshift galaxies. It includes cosmic age, lookback time, comoving distance, luminosity distance, angular-diameter distance, distance modulus, and angular scale. Conventions follow Hogg (1999); cf. the Wright (2006) calculator. Computed in-browser with JavaScript and validated against Astropy: all quantities agree to better than 0.01% for z ≤ 20 (and to ≲0.07% out to the last-scattering surface, z ≈ 1089).

Developed by Zihao Wu (Harvard) with assistance from Claude Code. Scientific correctness has been reviewed and approved by Zihao Wu. Source code is available on GitHub.
